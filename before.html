Before: Inefficient Alarm Interface

Many alarm interfaces force users through too many steps: selecting a mode, opening a submenu, scrolling, and confirming on multiple screens. This results in higher mental load and unnecessary clicks.

Problems with Old Design

  • Too many steps to set a simple time
  • No clear confirmation
  • Small inputs / hard to tap
  • No notification feedback
  • No emergency stop option
